### Advanced

OpenFHE-specific deep features — CKKS bootstrapping for refreshing deep
circuits past their multiplicative depth, and BinFHE boolean circuits
over encrypted bits. The statistical-application demos that previously
lived here (MLE, Cox regression, CVXR consensus ADMM, and their DP
variants) have been moved to the companion homomorpheR package (version
\>= 1.0), the natural home for stats-application demonstrations layered
on top of openfhe.R.
